WaiWai Check 11/22 (English)

 March 10, 2024

WaiWai Check 11/22 (わいわいチェック 11/22) is a Satellaview game.

The ROM was originally dumped through the effort of several people and published on the Satellablog.


Roms (Japanese and English)


Japanese ROM English Patch
On Satellablog ROM CRC32: 701009F1
Download Wai Wai Check (11-22) (English).bps

The game

The game is a “spot the difference” game.
You have an animated image on the left and the mirrored version on the right with 5 differences.
Moving the cursor around you have to find the differences, each worth different amount of points.

It is similar to other Waiwai Check games previously dumped. (See Other Waiwai games below)

The exclusive stage of this release is called Taishō Roman. According to wikipedia: Taishō Roman (Japanese: 大正ロマン, 大正浪漫) refers to the cultural and intellectual movement of Japanese Romanticism during the Taishō era, influenced by European Romanticism.


Stage and Game Modes

Stage Mode

Time Attack
You have 3 minutes total. You must find 3 differences to complete each round.
You can press X to skip a round (Only 3 times per game).

Thinking Challenge
You have 3 minutes per round to find 3 differences.
You get a special message on round 25, 50, 100, 125, 150, 175 and 200.
When the timer runs out, you get a postcard with a password encoding your score.

Accurate Mode
You have 3 minutes per round to find X differences (At least 1 in round 1, 2 in round 2 and so on). From round 5 and on, you must find all differences every round. This mode ends at round 50.
