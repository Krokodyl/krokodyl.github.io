Parabo's Game Quiz I

 October 7, 2024

Parabo’s Game Quiz is a homebrew game for the Satellaview (the Super Famicom extension).
It is a quiz where you have to identify NES games from a blurry screenshot.


Download

Music + SFX SFX only
Parabo’s Game Quiz I.bs Parabo’s Game Quiz I (NO BGM).bs

Gameplay



Each round you are granted 20 credits to spend on hints in order to help you identify the game.


Hint Effect
Unblur Makes the image slightly unblurry. Can be used up to 6 times to reveal the image.
Show MC Adds the main character to the image (affected by the blur)
Show NPC Adds enemies or objects to the image (affected by the blur)
Year Adds the release year to the info panel
Genre Adds the game genre to the info panel
Developer Adds the game studio to the info panel

When you’re ready to make a guess, you must choose the title from a list of games (all EU/NA releases for this edition). A correct guess converts your unused credits into score. A wrong guess takes 1 credit away (the round ends if you have no credit left). On the image screen, you can also hold SELECT to end the round early and score 0 points.



After 12 rounds, you get a postcard with your final score and a password for authentication in the fashion of many Satellaview games.




The game is named after Parabo, one of the two Satellaview mascots. (Satebo: ) and Parabo: ) \


Music

As silly as possible, Getaway, Lounging round
Composed by K. Jose with some re-arrangement to the SNES technical limitations.
