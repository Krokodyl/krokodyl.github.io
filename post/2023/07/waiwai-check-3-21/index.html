WaiWai Check 03/21 (English)

 July 8, 2023

Waiwai Check 3/21 (わいわいチェック 3/21) is a Satellaview game.

The ROM was originally dumped through the effort of several people and published on the Satellablog.

As the game was packed together with another one, I took the liberty to extract the game and rebuild a working pack with the game alone.


Roms (Japanese and English)

The game

The game is a “spot the difference” game.
You have an animated image on the left and the mirrored version on the right with 5 differences.
Moving the cursor around you have to find the differences, each worth different amount of points.

The Waiwai Check games were part of the radio show Hikaru Ijuin’s King of After School (伊集院光の放課後の王様, Ijuin Hikaru no Houkago no Ousama ) hosted by Hikaru Ijūin.

King of After School was recorded at St. Giga’s studio in Jingūmae, Shibuya until February 1996.
They moved the show to the Nintendo Tokyo Building studio in Asakusabashi, Taitō in March 1996.
We can see that change in the address on the end game postcard.
On the end game postcard, you can press Up and Down to scroll down the message.

There is a rom for a game “Waiwai Check 3-7” going around but it’s not working (It’s missing at least 25% of the data).
Looking at the content, it has stages called 温泉旅行 (Onsen Trip) and 平安絵巻 (Heian Period Emaki, the same stage as in the Waiwai Check 3/21 broadcast).


Stage and Game Modes

Stage Mode

Time Attack
You have 3 minutes total. You must find 3 differences to complete each round.
You can press X to skip a round (Only 3 times per game).

Thinking Challenge
You have 3 minutes per round to find 3 differences.
You get a special message on round 25, 50, 100, 125, 150, 175 and 200.
When the timer runs out, you get a postcard with a password encoding your score.

Accurate Mode
You have 3 minutes per round to find X differences (At least 1 in round 1, 2 in round 2 and so on). From round 5 and on, you must find all differences every round. This mode ends at round 50.
